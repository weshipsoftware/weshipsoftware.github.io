Agile Project

Turn that million dollar idea in your notebook into shipping software.

We'll work with you to come up with a project plan and give you our best-effort timeline estimates.

Then we'll work with you on designing, building, testing, and deploying your project.

We like to work in 1 to 3 week iterations, delivering small, working pieces of software at each step until the project is complete and you're ready to onboard customers.

We don't do long contracts. Instead, you'll pay us with each iteration. That way you're involved in the feedback loop — making product adjustments as we go without needing to negotiate scope changes. It'll lead to a better final product on a more predicatable timeline.
